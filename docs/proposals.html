What This Shows

For each sample, SAM 3's DETR decoder produces ~200 candidate masks ranked by confidence. We show the top 10 alongside the Semantic Segmentation Head output. This reveals whether the best mask is ranked #1, or buried deeper in the proposal list.

Green border = high GT IoU. Red border = low GT IoU. The Semantic Seg mask (green overlay) provides a dense alternative that doesn't depend on proposal ranking.
