About Sa2VA

Sa2VA (Segment Anything 2 with Visual Assistant) merges SAM2 with a VLM (Qwen3-VL) into a single end-to-end model. Given an image and a prompt, it generates text with [SEG] tokens that trigger SAM2 to produce segmentation masks — all in one forward pass.

Unlike the Qwen3+SAM3 pipeline (where Qwen describes and SAM3 segments separately), Sa2VA autonomously decides what to segment. It consistently produces a single mask per prompt; the second texture is the complement (1 − mask).

Result: Qwen3+SAM3 wins by +20.2 mIoU points

The two-model pipeline (Qwen3 VL describes + SAM3 segments) at 92.9% mIoU significantly outperforms Sa2VA's single-model approach at 72.7% mIoU. The specialized pipeline leverages SAM3's Semantic Seg Head for dense pixel-level prediction, while Sa2VA is designed for object-level referring segmentation and struggles with texture regions.
